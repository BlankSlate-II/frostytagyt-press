Why fake links get rejected

TikTok checks whether publications are real, established outlets with editorial standards — not self-made pages or unknown blogs created for verification. Submitting fabricated coverage can get your application denied or your account flagged. The 4 links below must be real articles written by real publications that mention you.

What TikTok Wants in Each Link

  • Published by an independent outlet (not your own website)
  • Not sponsored or paid placement
  • Mentions you by name or your @frostytagyt handle
  • From a site with real traffic, editorial team, and history
  • Related to your field (gaming, VR, TikTok creators, etc.)
